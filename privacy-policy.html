Privacy Policy for Offens!ve Blogs

At Offens!ve Blogs, I am committed to safeguarding your privacy while you interact with my website, sakibulalikhan.pages.dev. This Privacy Policy explains how I handle your information and outlines the steps I take to protect your privacy.

Effective Date: 30 November, 2024.

Information Collection and Use

As the owner of a personal blog, I do not collect any personally identifiable information from you unless you voluntarily provide it. I do not require you to register, subscribe, or log in to access the content on my blog.

However, non-personally identifiable information such as your IP address, browser type, and website activity may be automatically collected through cookies when you visit my site. These cookies are used solely for the purpose of improving your browsing experience and analyzing traffic patterns on the site.

Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device that enable websites to remember your preferences and track your activity. On this site, cookies help me understand how visitors use my website, allowing me to improve its performance and content. You can find more information about the specific cookies used on my site and how they are used in my Cookie Policy.

Third-Party Services

While I do not collect or store personal data, I do use a few third-party services that may set cookies or collect anonymized data. These third-party services help me improve the functionality of the site, analyze traffic, and ensure performance and security.

The third-party services I use are:

  • Google Analytics: A service that helps me understand how visitors interact with my website, such as what pages are visited or how long users stay on the site. Google Analytics uses cookies to gather anonymous data to help me improve the site. Learn more about Google Analytics privacy practices.

  • Microsoft Clarity: A tool that records user interactions with my website, such as clicks, scrolls, and mouse movements, to improve user experience. The data collected is anonymized. Learn more about Microsoft Clarity’s privacy practices.

  • Buy Me a Coffee: A platform that allows visitors to support my blog. If you interact with the Buy Me a Coffee widget, they may collect information related to your interaction. However, I do not have access to this data. Learn more about Buy Me a Coffee privacy practices.

  • Cloudflare: A service that provides security and performance optimization for my site. Cloudflare may use cookies for security purposes and to improve website performance. Learn more about Cloudflare’s privacy practices.

These third-party services may collect and use cookies to track anonymous data and improve the functionality of the website. Please review their privacy policies to understand how they handle your data.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have control over how cookies are used on your device. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to accept, reject, or delete cookies at any time. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on this website, but you can still browse without cookies if you prefer.

For more information on how to manage cookies, visit All About Cookies.

Data Security

Since I do not collect or store personally identifiable information, the primary data collected by third-party services (like Google Analytics or Microsoft Clarity) is anonymous. However, these services implement security measures to protect your data. Please be aware that I do not have direct access to any of your personal data, as it is handled by these third-party platforms.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in the way I operate the website or comply with new legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the Effective Date will be revised accordingly. Please check back regularly to stay informed about how I am protecting your privacy.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how I handle your data, feel free to contact me via my Contact page.
